About Us

Our Philosophy

We are passionate about working with exceptional founders who are building remarkable companies. While being Founders-first, our philosophy centres around putting founders at the forefront, supporting their vision, and backing their ideas with our commitment and dedication.

Think of us as an extended team bringing Plug-and-Play fundraising capabilities allowing founders to leverage our expertise around storytelling, market research, pitching, and accessing long-tail VC globally, thus creating huge tangible value.

Thesis:

We look at Seed stage (early revenue) to Series B+ ($5-10Mn revenue run rate).

Our preferred round size ranges between $1-10Mn.

We focus on technology-driven sectors including FinTech, HealthTech, SaaS, and Consumer Tech.

Why Work With Us?

We are problem solvers

Fundraising is way beyond connecting. We focus on solving the raise for founders through brainstorming, building compelling storylines, strategizing fundraising, A/B testing, and bringing unique resources to achieve exceptional outcomes.

We believe in long-term partnership

We partner early and support our founders through their valuation journey. Our commitment ensures lasting relationships built on trust, mutual value creation, and success.

We partner as an investor

We invest our time, resources, and network in our founders. Our focus is on adding value beyond capital, including strategic advice and meaningful introductions.

We take it personally

We view your success as our success. With a hands-on approach, we ensure that we’re aligned with your goals and dedicated to achieving them together.
